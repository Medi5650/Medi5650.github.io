Social implications

Low living wages have an effect on households as a whole, the low wages leave little room for any kind of spending. Poverty and being exposed to high levels of stress at a young age can have effects on a person’s health. Which include but are not limited to:

  • Mental illness
  • Risky behavior
  • Poor health in general
As a society we must relearn and let go of our prejudices or we may never be able to move forward and grow. It is up to us to strive to do better.

Political implications

This is an issue that is spread way beyond the borders of the United States of America. There are no limits to the effect that social justice inequality could have. It can cause distrust from the general population towards the active government. This distrust could lead to rebellious actions to impose the rule of the government to enact change in the society itself. We see this happen quite often, especially last year with all the power behind the Black Lives Matter movement and the abortion protest in Texas. When the government imposes oppressive laws or doesn’t help in a time of injustice the people will recognize that and fight back.
