Environmental Trends
One major environmental issue that we have been and are continuing to face is overpopulation. As the population of the human race continues to rise in combination with being able to live to quite an old age, we face many problems. To start off we are running out of areas to habit, we can not continue to the deforestation of the world in order to keep building areas for people to live or it may kill us. We are over farming in order to feed all these people I believe that the best way to go about both of these issues is building up. Building ecofriendly housing that is meant to last and is made with resources from the local environment instead of building apartment complexes just to get them built fast. These building could also use vertical farming and garden on the building that uses grey water as the water source as a way to combat deforestation and bring cleaner air for people. Unfortunately, due to overpopulation we are also running out of natural resources needed in or to sustain the amount of people we have. It has also contributed to water pollution which I believe can be solved through proper water and waste management. It has even begun to affect our economy widening the wage gap and having a detrimental effect on the poor. This is something we must really focus on trying to solve because if something is not done soon the effects will be permanent and there will be little to nothing we can do.
