About Me
I am Junior here at UNCO with Business Administration major with a concentration in Marketing and minoring in Digital Marketing. A few of my interest include but are not limited to painting, reading, cooking, fashion, and making old people angry. I tend to paint whenever I am feeling emotional and you can really see and feel whatever emotion I’m feeling, I see it as a form of journaling. Usually when painting I enjoy using multiple mediums to play with tones and add texture and dimension to the piece. I recently started reading more often because I don’t enjoy getting my ideas from the internet there is something more satisfying about reading something and interpreting it your own way to reach a conclusion that is unique to you. Cooking for myself is nice but I love to cook for a group I see it as a form of love language, here is something I made for you to enjoy; its quite fun. I see fashion not only as a form of self-expression but as a form of art as well, painting is another form of self-expression.
